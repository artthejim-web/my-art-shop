About Me

A young visual artist & cinematic photographer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I work in charcoal, graphite, and colored pencils—specializing in hyperrealistic portraits, animal portraits, sketches, and custom commissions. Every piece is built with patience and precision. If you can imagine it, I can draw it.
